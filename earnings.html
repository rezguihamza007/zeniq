Career Tiers (Bonus Payouts)

With each change of position at SAFIR, you obtain bonuses, the latter are paid for 6 months. With a cumulative turnover of €100,000 (performance of referrals included) the Sapphire status gives you a bonus of €3,000, i.e. a total of €18,000 paid directly into your SAFIR wallet.

Once you have obtained a new status, you can no longer lose it, so it is acquired until the next one.

A coefficient has been assigned to you for the Sharepoints, the latter will be taken into account to calculate your bonus pool.

Bonus Pool / Performance bonus

Each month, SAFIR donates 10% of its income to a "pool", calculated according to the number of people and the turnover that the company has put in this pool, SAFIR will donate an income by calculating the number of stars ongoing to the entire eligible community.

The number of stars varies according to the position that each has reached at the time of taking the snapshot of the accounts.

In August 2021, one star was worth €195. In September 2021, it rose to €263.

This pool bonus is in addition to the career bonus.
